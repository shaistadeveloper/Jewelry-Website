The Zaviya Legacy

Founded in 2025, ZAVIYA is more than a jewelry brand — it's a celebration of Pakistan's rich craftsmanship and contemporary design. Each piece is handcrafted by master artisans using ethically sourced gemstones and recycled gold. Our name "Zaviya" means "perspective" in Persian, symbolizing a unique viewpoint on timeless beauty.

We believe jewelry tells stories of love, heritage, and individuality. From minimalist everyday elegance to opulent bridal sets, ZAVIYA brings your dreams to life with uncompromising quality and passion.
